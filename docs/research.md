# Research

## Related Projects Review

Review at least one similar existing project.

For each project, include:

- Project name
- Main features
- What your team learned from it

## Technology Review

Compare candidate solutions and justify your final choices.

Cover relevant aspects such as:

- Possible technical solution paths
- Devices (if applicable)
- Algorithms (if applicable)
- Programming languages, frameworks, libraries, and APIs

## Summary of Technical Decisions

Provide a concise summary of your final technical decisions and trade-offs.

## References

Use IEEE style references and IEEE in-text citations.

## Development Checklist (From COMP0016 Part 2)

- Review at least one related project with learning points.
- Compare candidate technologies and justify your selection.
- Summarize technical decisions clearly.
- Provide IEEE-format references.
- Use IEEE-style in-text citations.
